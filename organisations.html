Timescaping is a 120-minute session where your team steps 100 years into the future. At that distance, the present looks different in ways that matter.

Not to predict anything. The further you go, the less anyone knows. That's when people stop performing and start imagining. That's when perspectives emerge that wouldn't normally get space.

The day after, people often show up to meetings with different questions.
