WHAT IS TIMESCAPING

A 120-MINUTE SESSION WHERE YOUR TEAM STEPS 100 YEARS INTO THE FUTURE.

At that distance, the present looks different in ways that matter.

Not to predict anything. The further you go, the less anyone knows. Far enough that nobody has the right answer.
Far enough that expertise stops being a shield.
Far enough that the stories we tell ourselves about what is inevitable start to loosen. That's when people stop performing and start imagining. That's when perspectives emerge that wouldn't normally get space.

Guided by an experienced facilitator, participants move through a series of structured moments that open up imagination about possible futures, rooted in their own expertise, worldviews, and what emerges between the people in the room. Timescape cards introduce the initial themes and gradually open the conversation outward. Sessions often end with participants creating their own cards, a starting point for the work that follows. The day after, people often show up to meetings with different questions.
