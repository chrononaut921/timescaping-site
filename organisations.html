Most teams think about the future regularly. Strategy sessions, planning days, quarterly reviews.

But all of it happens from inside the present. The same assumptions, the same reference points, the same people thinking in the same ways.

The world is changing faster than that.

And teams that can only think from inside what exists don't see what's coming until it's already happening.

Timescaping changes the conditions under which that conversation happens.

WHAT IS TIMESCAPING

WHAT IS
TIMESCAPING

Timescaping is a 120-minute session where your team steps 100 years into the future. At that distance, the present looks different in ways that matter.

Not to predict anything. The further you go, the less anyone knows. That's when people stop performing and start imagining. That's when perspectives emerge that wouldn't normally get space.

Amortality. Android rights. Consciousness upload. Challenging AI inevitability. New social contracts. And other themes crafted specifically around your sector.

The day after, people often show up to meetings with different questions.

Teams that have practiced thinking in different futures don't freeze when one arrives.
